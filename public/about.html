About Us

Who We Are

Fleurier was founded by a group of passionate women who share one thing in common — a deep love for flowers.

What started as a simple hobby blossomed into a mission: to spread joy, beauty, and elegance through stunning floral arrangements. Every bouquet we create is crafted with care, creativity, and a personal touch, because for us, flowers aren't just products — they're expressions of love.

Whether it's for a celebration, a surprise, or just to make someone smile, we're here to make every moment bloom.
